# AI Frontier Technology Researcher AI前沿技术研究员
China Mobile（Hong Kong）Innovation Research Institute
香港特别行政区 香港
- Job Responsibilities:
Focusing on the application of large models, leveraging intelligent agents (Agents) to drive key technological advancements in B2B and B2C applications:
Responsible for the end-to-end development of large model-based intelligent agents (Agents), including but not limited to data processing, training, optimization, and deployment.
Explore and implement the latest machine learning algorithms and AI models, optimizing Agents’ core capabilities such as memory, planning, actions, and tools to enhance performance and efficiency in real-world user scenarios.
Lead the deployment of large model-based Agents and conduct related research and development for practical applications.
Stay up to date with cutting-edge technologies and participate in key research and breakthroughs in large model-based Agents.
Complete other tasks assigned by the leadership.

- 围绕大模型的落地，以智能体（Agents）为抓手，推进大模型在toB和toC应用中的核心技术攻坚:
负责大模型智能体（Agents）的全流程工作，包括但不限于数据处理、训练、优化和部署；
探索和实施最新的机器学习算法和AI模型，负责Agents的优化，包括但不限于memory, plan, action, tools等核心能力，提升Agents的在用户场景下的性能和效率；

紧跟前沿技术，参与大模型Agents关键技术研究和突破；
完成领导交办的其他任务。

- Job Requirements:
Master’s degree or above in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, Big Data, or related fields.
Strong programming skills, proficient in Python and C++, with experience in deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch and TensorFlow. Capable of independently designing, developing, and testing algorithms.
Familiarity with large model training and optimization techniques, including but not limited to Prompt Engineering, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT).
Understanding of Agent frameworks, with business problem modeling capabilities; hands-on experience in Agent-based applications is a plus.
Proficiency in natural language processing, machine learning, and deep learning techniques.
Strong problem-solving skills, passion for AI technology innovation, and enthusiasm for AI product development. Excellent teamwork and communication skills, with the ability to work effectively in interdisciplinary teams.
Strong communication and coordination abilities, with fluency in English (listening, speaking, reading, and writing). Ability to conduct daily research work in English.
